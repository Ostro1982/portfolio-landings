# background

I spent fifteen years as a professional poker and trading card player. That meant making thousands of decisions a week with incomplete information, calculating expected value on the fly, and learning to spot what actually moves the needle.

Now I build websites the same way: every "pixel", every "copy line", every "CTA" has to earn its place. If it doesn't push conversion, it doesn't ship.

# philosophy

I don't sell pages. I sell client-acquisition machines that happen to look good.
