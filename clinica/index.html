Our practice

Care that respects
the face you have

For the past six years, Lumen has built a different kind of aesthetic clinic in Palermo. One where every protocol begins with a conversation, every procedure is reviewed by a doctor, and every result aims to be invisible to everyone but you.

We do not chase trends. We do not perform on people who walk in asking for a celebrity's face. We work the way classical medicine works: gradually, conservatively, and only when it actually serves the person in front of us.

The best aesthetic work is the kind nobody notices, but everybody feels. Dra. Lucia Marini · Medical Director

A note from our director

We believe

I founded Lumen because I was tired of watching aesthetic medicine become a race to the bottom. Patients arriving with reference photos of Instagram filters. Doctors competing on who could inject more product, faster, cheaper.

The face you were born with is the most beautiful version of you. My job is to take care of it. To slow down what time does, not to undo it. To bring back what you remember, not invent something you've seen on a screen.

If that is what you are looking for, we should talk.
